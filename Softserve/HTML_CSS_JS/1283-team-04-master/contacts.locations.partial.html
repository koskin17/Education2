Returns & Refunds

At NextLevel, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you’re not happy with a product, our returns and refunds process is simple and hassle-free.

Return Policy:

  • ✔️ Time Frame: You can return any item within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange.
  • ✔️ Condition: Products must be in their original packaging and in like-new condition. Items showing signs of use may be subject to a restocking fee.
  • ✔️ Process: To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team. We’ll provide you with a return authorization and instructions on how to send your item back.

Refunds:

  • ✔️ Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The refund will be issued to the original payment method.

Exchanges:

  • ✔️ If you’d like to exchange your item for a different product, please let us know when you contact us. We’ll do our best to accommodate your request.

Warranty Information

NextLevel is committed to providing high-quality products that you can rely on. That’s why we offer a comprehensive warranty on all our products.

Warranty Coverage:

    ✔️ Time Frame: We offer a standard warranty period of 12 months from the date of purchase.
  • ✔️ Eligibility: The warranty is applicable to products purchased directly from NextLevel in online and offline stores throughout the USA, ensuring that you receive genuine support.
  • ✔️ Support: During the warranty period, you have access to our dedicated customer support team, who are available to assist you with any warranty-related inquiries or claims.

What’s Covered:

  • ✔️ Defects in materials and workmanship and functional failures. We repair or replace products that exhibit defects or fail to function properly due to manufacturing issues.

What’s Not Covered:

    ✔️ Accidental damage, normal wear and tear, and unauthorized repairs are not covered by the warranty. This also applies to items such as batteries, cables, and other consumables.
